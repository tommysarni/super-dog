Frequently Asked Questions

Who made this site and why?

Hi! My name is Tommy Sarni and I'm the creator of this site. I built this because I've love dogs and am fascinated by all the different breeds and their unique personalities and skills. Also I built this because this searchable info is exactly what I would've have liked to use myself, so I hope you enjoy it!

What is this site?

There are hundreds of dog breeds out there and each of them have their own flavor of sizes, shapes, talents, and attitudes. This site's goal is to provide and organize this information to be easily accesible and useable!

Where is all the info sourced from?

All the research information came from the sources down below. They are great and I recommend to all those who are interested! All the images are sourced from resources under creative commons agreements. They came from the Stanford Dogs Dataset, pexels.com, pixabay.com, and Wikimedia Commons.

  • The Complete Dog Breed Book: Choose the perfect dog for you. (2020). Dorling Kindersley Ltd.
  • Coile, D. C., & Earle-Bridges, M. (2016). Encyclopedia of dog breeds. Barron's, Credo Reference.
  • The American Kennel Club website: https://www.akc.org/
